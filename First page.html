75 Soft Challenge

The "75 Soft Challenge" is a personal development challenge aimed at improving physical and mental well-being. Participants commit to a daily routine that includes a 30-minute workout, eating home-cooked meals, limiting eating out and alcohol consumption, and reading 10 pages of a book. The challenge emphasizes consistency and self-discipline, with the goal of fostering healthy habits and personal growth.

RULES

  • 30 Mins 5x/week Workout
  • Eat homecook meals
  • Eat out 1x week
  • Drink max 1x week
  • Read 10 pages daily
